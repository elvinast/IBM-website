Among our teachers of programming are founders and current employees of the world's leading IT corporations. We are not just teachers, we are programming practitioners with huge experience.

In addition to teaching programming, we help with employment and constantly advise our students on further development. In our school, you can learn not only programming, but also other areas of programming, such as web development and Data Science.

How long is the bootcamp?

Monday - Friday for 14 weeks.

Your days will include lectures, working sessions, workshops and breakout group work. Some afternoons we'll offer the option to for career talks after hours and mentoring opportunities.

What development skills will I learn?

It depends on the course type that you'll choose.

For example, if you'll choose Game Development path, then, firstly, you'll learn basics of Python programming language, then we will continue on the PyGame library that will help you to build your own games.

How can I prepare before bootcamp begins?

Once you've been accepted into Codium we'll be reaching out to you for best ways to prepare with exercises recommended by our instructors

In the meantime, Stanford's Computer Science 101 is a great place to start. It's free and very helpful.
